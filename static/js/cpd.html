Continuing Professional Development

Making learning easier for your developers

Continuing Professional Development is a feature of all professions and even more so in the Web Development industry.

Technology changes so quickly. It can be hard for your developers to find both the time and energy to learn new subjects, particularly if there are no structured and organised learning resources.

Furthermore, the expression 'a ton of ore for an ounce of gold ' rings so true. Time spent learning a new technology can be vey inefficient and full of frustration and stress.

I can help with this by providing micro-training, 1-3 hour training labs that have the following advantages:

  • Fully researched and up to date training.
  • Boiler plates with all the heavy lifting already done.
  • Ongoing support.
  • Small oingoing sessions that can be done before work or as extended lunch breaks.
  • Great cost and time savings for you and your company.

The workshops use a series of 'Step by Step' examples combined with some theory. It is very much a code lab with attendees able to take away a fully functioning PWA. The focus is on rapid, efficent learning and development with ready made scaffolding.

Essential JavaScript for WordPress developers is also included where needed. A resource sheet is given with links to excellent resoruces for learning and boiler plates.
